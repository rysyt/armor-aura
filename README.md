# ArmorAura

A Fabric mod that adds passive effects and buffs based on the armor trims you're wearing.

## Trim Effects

### Snout Trim

Equip the snout trim on any piece of armor to unlock two piglin-related effects:

**Piglin Aggro Immunity**
Piglins will not attack you, even without golden armor. The snout trim signals to them that you're one of their own.

**Lucky Trades (25% chance)**
When you throw gold ingots at a piglin, there is a 25% chance of triggering a lucky trade:
- The piglin admires the gold for ~9 seconds instead of the usual ~6 seconds
- The barter result draws from a boosted loot table:
  - All rare items (Soul Speed books, iron boots, fire resistance potions, ender pearls, etc.) are 4x more likely than vanilla
  - Small chance of receiving 3-4 diamonds
  - Junk items (gravel, blackstone, nether brick, etc.) remain at vanilla rates

Without the snout trim, piglin bartering is 100% vanilla — no changes.
